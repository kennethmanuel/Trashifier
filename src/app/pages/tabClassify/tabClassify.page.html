Welcome to Trashifier

 What is Trashifier?
Trashifier is an app created to help people recognize their garbage type and be more educated about sorting their trash.
How do I use Trashifier?
Simply click the Start Classifying button, take an image of a trash that you didn't know its type. Even if you know what type it is, maybe you'll learn a things or two that you didn't know about your Trash type 😉️.

Note that while Trashifier can classify most of common municipal solid waste, but 'most' means not everything. It can also guess what type of plastic your trash is if it happens to be a plastic 😯️🙀️.
How does Trashifier work?
Trashifier work by using a machine learning model specifically designed to classify a trash, hence the name Trashifier.

The app simply sends the image to a server in which the classifier is deployed. The server then tries to predict and sent back the result. Note that Trashifier does not collect any of the image sent, for more info check Trashifier's privacy policy.
